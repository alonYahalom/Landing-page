Diamond Pressure

The Diamond Pressure is one of the quietest massage guns on the market. Its 24-volt, high-torque brushless motor keeps noise between 35 and 55 decibels. Even on the highest of its 16 different speed settings, it’s quieter than the lowest setting of most other massage guns. Its battery life is also competitive, clocking in around three hours for one full charge. And at 1.5 pounds, it’s a pretty lightweight option as well. Plus, it comes with a convenient carrying case so you don’t have to worry about misplacing its various attachments.
